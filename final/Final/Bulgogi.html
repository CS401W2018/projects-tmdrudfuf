Ingredient

Main

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Onion
  • Green onion
  • Sesame seeds
  • Carrot

Sauce

  • Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Mirim
  • Minced garlic
  • Minced ginger
  • Minced green onion
  • Sesame oil

Preps

  • Seasoning the beef with sauce and let it rest
  • Slice onion, green onion and Carrot

Cooking

  1. Stir-fry the marinated meat in a little oil
  2. Once the meat is half-cooked, add the vegetables and stir-fry together
  3. Add black pepper to taste
  4. Finally, top with sesame seeds
