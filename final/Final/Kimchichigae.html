Ingredient

Main

  • Kimchi
  • Pork
  • Broth
  • Green Onion
  • Onion

Sauce

  • Minced Garlic
  • Red pepper powder
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper
  • Cooking oil
  • Kimchi juice

Preps

  • Slice the onion and chop the green onion
  • Cut the kimchi
  • Cut the pork into bite-sized pieces
  • And then toss the meat with the sauce

Cooking

  1. Stir-fry the marinated pork in a pot until it's half cooked
  2. And add kimchi and stir-fry
  3. Pour broth, and cover the pot and simmer over low heat until it bubbles gently
  4. Finally, add sliced onions and green onions, and bring to a boil
