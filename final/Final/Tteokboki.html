BIBIMBAP

Bibimbap Bulgogi Kimchichigae Tteokboki Home Information

Ingredient

Main

  • Tteokboki Rice cake
  • Fish cake
  • Green onion
  • Onion
  • Sesame seeds

Sauce

  • Korean pepper paste
  • Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Red pepper powder
  • minced garlic

Preps

  • Slice the onion and chop the green onion
  • Separate the rice cakes and rinse them one by one under cold water
  • Cut fish cake into bite-sized pieces

Cooking

  1. Boil the rice cakes in a pot of water
  2. Once the water boils, add the fish cakes and sauce, then stir
  3. Finish the dish by adding the remaining vegetables and cooking until everything is heated through
  4. Top with sesame seeds
