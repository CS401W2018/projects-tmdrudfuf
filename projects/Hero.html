My hero - bak Jongwon

He is one of South Korea's leading culinary researchers and entrepreneurs. He has appeared on various entertainment programs and is loved by many people for sharing his recipes and food business know-how. In particular, he has contributed to the "popularization of cooking" by developing recipes that make difficult dishes simple and delicious.

culinary researcher
-he has made cooking accessible to the public by preseinting simple and delicious recipes
restaurant entrepreneurs
-he got his start in the restaurant business by founding The Born Korea and now runs a variety of franchises.
Broadcaster
-he has gained popularity through his appearances on a variety of entertainment programs, not just cooking programs.
