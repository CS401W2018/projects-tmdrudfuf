My hero - bak Jongwon

He is one of South Korea's leading culinary researchers and entrepreneurs. He has appeared on various entertainment programs and is loved by many people for sharing his recipes and food business know-how. In particular, he has contributed to the "popularization of cooking" by developing recipes that make difficult dishes simple and delicious.
